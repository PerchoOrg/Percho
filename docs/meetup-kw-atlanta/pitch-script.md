# Percho — KW Atlanta Meetup Pitch Script

**Tone reminder:** first-touch, friendly, curious. We are here to *listen* to
agents, not close them. Prefer questions over claims. If an agent asks for the
pitch, use the shortest version that fits the moment.

---

## 30-second version (elevator / hallway)

> Hey, I'm [FOUNDER NAME] — we're building **Percho**, a swipe-based video
> feed for Atlanta home listings. Think of it like TikTok, but every video is
> a real home a real agent is selling.
>
> The pitch to agents is simple: **you just enter the address, we auto-fill
> all listing info from FMLS, all you need to do is upload one video, and
> Percho precisely delivers it to Atlanta home-buyers.**
>
> It's free during beta and every lead goes 100% to you. Would love to hear
> how you're currently reaching buyers — got 60 seconds?

---

## 2-minute version (booth / small circle)

> I'm [FOUNDER NAME], founder of **Percho** (percho.co). We're an
> Atlanta-first, video-first way for home-buyers to discover listings —
> imagine scrolling TikTok, except every clip is a home for sale by a local
> agent.
>
> **What agents actually do on Percho:** you just enter the address, we
> auto-fill all listing info from FMLS, all you need to do is upload one
> video, and Percho precisely delivers it to Atlanta home-buyers. That's
> it. No listing form to fill out. No writing descriptions. No lead-gen
> upsells.
>
> A few things that matter to agents I've talked to:
>
> - **Free during beta.** No per-lead fees, no ZIP-code auctions.
> - **Non-exclusive.** Keep your listings on Zillow, Redfin, wherever — we
>   don't ask you to pull anything.
> - **Every lead is 100% yours.** We don't resell buyer contacts. When a
>   buyer taps "contact agent," it goes straight to you.
> - **Your video, your brand.** Your face, your name, your license — the
>   buyer knows exactly who they're going to tour with.
>
> We're an MVP — Atlanta only, FMLS only, small agent roster. That's on
> purpose. I'd rather get 20 Atlanta agents thrilled than 2,000 lukewarm
> ones nationwide.
>
> Honestly, I mostly want to learn tonight — what's working for you, what's
> broken about the platforms you use now. If Percho sounds interesting,
> there's a QR on our card that goes to the agent waitlist. No pressure.

## 5-minute version (sit-down conversation / mini-demo)

**Opening (30s)**

> Thanks for a minute. I'm [FOUNDER NAME], and this is [TEAMMATE NAME]. We
> run **Percho** — percho.co. Before I pitch anything, can I ask: how
> are you finding buyers right now? Zillow leads? Referrals? Social?

*(Listen. Take notes. Match the pitch to what they said.)*

**What Percho is (60s)**

> Percho is a swipe-based video feed for US home listings — Atlanta first.
> A buyer opens the app, swipes vertically like TikTok or Reels, and every
> video is a real, live listing from a real Atlanta agent. Tap once to see
> price, beds/baths, square footage, address; tap again to reach the agent.
>
> We started as a tool for the Chinese-speaking buyer community and
> pivoted on June 22nd this year to serve *all* US home-buyers — because the
> discovery problem isn't language, it's format. Buyers don't want to read a
> 40-photo grid. They want to feel the house in 30 seconds.

**What agents actually do (60s)**

> The agent workflow is deliberately boring — that's the point.
>
> **You just enter the address, we auto-fill all listing info from FMLS,
> all you need to do is upload one video, and Percho precisely delivers
> it to Atlanta home-buyers.**
>
> Under the hood we pull from FMLS via Bridge Interactive (their official
> RESO Web API vendor), so price, beds, baths, sqft, MLS number, photos —
> all populated. You shoot one walkthrough video on your phone, upload it,
> and you're done. Buyers who match that neighborhood, price band, and
> style see it in their feed.

**What matters to agents (90s)**

> Five things I want to be upfront about:
>
> 1. **Free during beta.** No signup fee, no monthly, no per-lead. When we
>    do introduce pricing later, beta agents get grandfathered terms — I'll
>    put that in writing.
>
> 2. **Non-exclusive, always.** Percho is additive. Keep every current
>    channel — Zillow, Redfin, Realtor.com, your own site — nothing to pull
>    down. If we ever require exclusivity, that would be a different
>    product with a different name.
>
> 3. **Leads go 100% to you.** We are *not* a lead-resale business. When a
>    buyer contacts you through Percho, that contact is yours — we don't
>    sell it, share it, or route it to a "premier agent" in the ZIP.
>
> 4. **Your video, your brand.** Every listing card shows your name, your
>    photo, your license number, your brokerage. Buyer knows who they're
>    talking to before they tap.
>
> 5. **You own the video you upload.** We get a license to display it on
>    Percho while your listing is active — that's it. Take it down
>    anytime.

**Where we are honestly (30s)**

> We're an MVP. Atlanta only, FMLS only, iOS + web today. VOW-side data
> access is on the roadmap; right now we operate under IDX-style display
> rules. Small agent roster on purpose — I want the first 20 to help
> shape it. If that sounds like your kind of thing, great. If not, I still
> want your feedback tonight.

**Ask (30s)**

> Two things:
>
> 1. Would you be up for a 15-minute call next week to walk through the
>    agent side and tell me where it breaks?
> 2. If nothing else, scan this QR — it goes to the agent waitlist at
>    percho.co/agents. Zero commitment.
>
> And now — if you'll indulge me — what's the single most annoying thing
> about Zillow leads for you right now?

*(Then shut up and take notes.)*
