## Three benefits (with icon suggestions)

Each icon: simple line-art, 2px stroke, brand accent color. Lucide-react
names in parens.

### 1. Zero data entry
**Icon:** `map-pin` (or `sparkles`)
Enter the address. FMLS auto-fills price, beds, baths, sqft, MLS #, and
photos. You add one video. That's the whole workflow.

### 2. Every lead is yours
**Icon:** `user-check` (or `mail`)
No lead resale. No premier-agent auction. When a buyer taps "contact
agent," it comes straight to your inbox and dashboard.

### 3. Your face, front and center
**Icon:** `video` (or `id-card`)
Every listing shows your name, photo, license, and brokerage. Buyers know
who they're touring with before they tap.

## 三大卖点

### 1. 零录入
输入地址即可，FMLS 自动填充价格、卧室、浴室、面积、MLS 编号和照片。
你只需要上传一段视频，就这么简单。

### 2. 客源 100% 归你
不做客源转售，不做"金牌经纪人"竞价。买家点击"联系经纪人"时，
信息直接进你的邮箱和后台。

### 3. 你的脸，你的品牌
每个房源卡都显示你的姓名、头像、执照编号和所属公司。
买家在点击之前就知道自己在和谁沟通。
