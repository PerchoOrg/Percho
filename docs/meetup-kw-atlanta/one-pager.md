## What is Vicinity?

Vicinity is a swipe-based video feed for Atlanta home listings — think TikTok,
but every clip is a real home, listed by a real local agent. Buyers scroll,
tap, and reach out to *you* directly.

**The agent workflow, in one sentence:**
You just enter the address, we auto-fill all listing info from FMLS, all you
need to do is upload one video, and Vicinity precisely delivers it to Atlanta
home-buyers.

## Why join now?

We're an Atlanta MVP with a small founding roster of agents. The first agents
in shape the product, get grandfathered pricing when beta ends, and own the
video presence in their neighborhoods before anyone else.

This is a **first-touch** intro — no contract, no fee, no exclusivity. Just an

---

## Three concrete benefits for agents

### 1. Zero data entry
Enter the address. FMLS (via Bridge Interactive) fills in price, beds,
baths, sqft, MLS #, and photos automatically. You upload one video. Done.

### 2. 100% of leads are yours
When a buyer taps "contact agent," it comes straight to your inbox and
dashboard. We do not resell, share, or auction leads. Ever.

### 3. Your face, your brand
Every listing card shows your name, photo, license, and brokerage. Buyers
know who they're touring with before they tap.

---

## What Vicinity is *not*

- **Not exclusive.** Keep your listings on Zillow, Redfin, everywhere.
- **Not a lead broker.** We don't stand between you and the buyer.
- **Not a brokerage.** We don't compete for your buyer. Your relationship,
  your commission.
- **Not asking for money during beta.** Free means free.
