# Marketing log

Daily record of parasitic distribution — Reddit / FB / Quora / Zillow-Realtor
comments where Percho (soft) mentions get planted alongside genuinely useful
answers to high-intent Atlanta relocation questions.

## Beachhead

**Atlanta metro.** Locked in 2026-07-19. Reason: Percho verified content
(subdivisions, k12 schools pipeline, KW Atlanta meetup outreach) is all
Atlanta-centric. Expansion to other metros waits until we have real
photo / listing content there.

## Layout

- `voice.md` — **the red-line file.** How our replies must sound (anti-AI
  tone rules). Every template links back to it. Read this first.
- `accounts.md` — 4-Reddit-account roster + 2 FB groups + Quora deferred.
  Handles / karma / status only. No credentials.
- `templates/` — reusable reply scaffolds (schools+family, ITP-vs-OTP,
  first-time buyer, remote worker lifestyle, retirement). Never
  copy-verbatim — always rewrite for the OP.
- `daily/YYYY-MM-DD.md` — one file per day. Setup actions, replies sent,
  karma snapshots, learnings.
- `inbox.md` (created Day 3) — manually curated pool of active reddit
  threads to reply to once accounts are warm.

## Weekly rhythm

- **Mon-Fri:** F5Bot emails come in → I triage → propose replies from
  templates → you approve → I post (from the right account) → log to
  `daily/`.
- **Every Sunday evening:** rollup at bottom of that day's log — karma
  gained per account, top 3 upvoted threads of the week, what tone worked,
  what got downvoted, adjustments to `voice.md` if needed.

## The playbook, condensed

1. **Age Reddit accounts 7-10 days on unrelated topics** before any
   housing reply. See `accounts.md` karma milestones.
2. **80% real info, 20% soft mention.** Info comes first. Percho mention
   is the last sentence, lowercase, admits incompleteness, offers a
   specific dm ("i'll dm you the p'tree corners page"), has an escape
   hatch ("or ignore this whole msg lol").
3. **Never `utm_*`.** Kills the link through Reddit's spam filter.
4. **Rewrite every reply for OP's actual budget / kids / commute.** Templates
   provide 10 data points per subdivision; a real reply uses 2-3.
5. **F5Bot hit → reply within 4 hours if in warm-account window.** Early
   replies dominate long-tail traffic; late replies are wasted.
6. **FB groups: 5 days lurk + like only. Never post your own thread.**
   Reply to others' posts, never author.
7. **1 Percho mention per day per account, max.** More = pattern-match.

## Non-negotiable voice rules

Repeated here because they're the reason 90% of AI-authored marketing
replies fail:

- Reply MUST read as typed by a real Atlanta resident. Info accuracy
  is second to voice.
- No hard-no vocab (see `voice.md` for the list).
- One uncertainty phrase per reply, minimum.
- Fragment sentences. Missing punctuation ok. Casual abbrevs.
- Persona-authentic opening — not "For your situation, …".

Full check in `voice.md`. Every template references it. Every reply
passes its checklist before posting.
