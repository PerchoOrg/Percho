Design decisions:
· Group labels (uppercase muted) turn the page into a settings surface — replaces the current "Account settings" mini-card that only held one line.
· Row list instead of pill-button stack — same click target, less visual weight, scales as we add settings.
· Sign out is its own card, always at the bottom, rose only on hover (no red-alert-on-load).
· Anon variant now offers Explore entry points too, not just login CTAs — Me tab isn't a dead-end for logged-out visitors.
· Buyer "My activity" fills the current void (Saved / Followed neighborhoods).
· Agent "My work" houses Public profile + Analytics + (future MLS import) — separate from Account so identity/auth/work are distinct.
